// 02 · METADAO & PLATFORM CONTEXT
The ownership coin thesis.
MetaDAO produces ownership coins: tokens with legal claim on the issuing entity, market-gated treasury spending, high float at launch with no insider tiers, and team unlocks gated on sustained price performance. The platform's products and fees follow from that asset-class definition.
STRUCTURAL BASELINE
Most crypto tokens have negative drift: opaque treasuries, foundation cash-outs, low float at launch with calendar-vesting insider allocations, and day-one CEX listings that exhaust marginal demand. Artificial scarcity inflates FDV; unlocks then bleed it out.
MetaDAO's ownership coins issue from a Cayman segregated portfolio bound by onchain governance. Treasury USDC sits under market oversight with monthly spending caps. If the token trades below NAV, holders can vote a buyback. If the team underdelivers, holders can vote a liquidation.
~40% high float at ICO, no private allocations, no insider tiers. Team performance package up to 50% of supply, structured as five tranches that unlock only at 2×, 4×, 8×, 16×, 32× ICO price (minimum 18-month cliff).

// NOVORA POV
The launchpad-native peer set frames META's multiple in apples-to-apples terms. With Q1 revenue printing at $886K versus the prior $556–700K Artemis-derived range, META now trades at 14.9× — a discount to BONK.fun (22.3×) and a meaningful premium to Pump.fun (2.2×) and Metaplex (6.5×). Both META and BONK.fun reflect structural premiums tied to disclosed token-level value accrual: futarchy treasury rights for META, BNKK's NASDAQ-listed 51% revenue interest for BONK.fun.
The flat MCap/revenue multiple still does not adjust for the rights differential within the set. META holders have a binding claim on platform-level treasury, the right to vote a buyback below NAV, and the right to liquidate. The Ranger precedent demonstrated those rights actually settle. BONK.fun's revenue routes to BONK via 50% buy/burn; META's routes to holders via futarchy. Both are defensible structures. The discount to BONK.fun reflects scale and revenue runway; the premium to Pump.fun reflects accrual mechanics.
